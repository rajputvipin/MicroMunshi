About Micomunshi

Empower Your Microfinance Operations

Micomunshi is a revolutionary micro-loan management platform designed specifically for microfinance institutions. Built by experts at Parnav Foundation, our ERP system streamlines every aspect of your lending operations.

From borrower onboarding to real-time collection tracking, Micomunshi provides you with the tools you need to scale your microfinance business efficiently and securely.

  • Seamless Borrower Onboarding
  • Real-Time Collection Tracking
  • Advanced Repayment Management
  • Comprehensive Reporting & Analytics
  • Secure Data Management
  • Mobile-Friendly Interface
  • Web & App Development
  • Sell / Purchase of Section 8 Microfinance Companies

Our Services

Borrower Onboarding

Streamlined and secure borrower registration process with KYC verification, document management, and digital identity verification.

Loan Management

Complete loan lifecycle management from application to disbursement, including interest calculations and flexible repayment schedules.

Real-Time Collections

Track collection and repayments in real-time with automated reminders, payment tracking, and default management.

Reporting & Analytics

Comprehensive reports and analytics dashboard for informed decision-making and performance monitoring.

Data Security

Bank-level security with encryption, regular backups, and compliance with international data protection standards.

24/7 Support

Dedicated customer support team available round-the-clock to assist with any technical or operational queries.

Web & App Development

Professional, high-performance websites and mobile apps tailored to your business needs, from design to deployment.

Sell / Purchase of Section 8 Microfinance Companies

Hassle-free facilitation of buying and selling Section 8 microfinance companies with full legal, compliance, and documentation support.
