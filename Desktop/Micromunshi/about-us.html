About Micomunshi

Empower Your Microfinance Operations

Micomunshi is a revolutionary micro-loan management platform designed specifically for microfinance institutions. Built by experts at Parnav Foundation, our ERP system streamlines every aspect of your lending operations.

From borrower onboarding to real-time collection tracking, Micomunshi provides you with the tools you need to scale your microfinance business efficiently and securely.

  • Seamless Borrower Onboarding
  • Real-Time Collection Tracking
  • Advanced Repayment Management
  • Comprehensive Reporting & Analytics
  • Secure Data Management
  • Mobile-Friendly Interface
  • Web & App Development
  • Sell / Purchase of Section 8 Microfinance Companies
